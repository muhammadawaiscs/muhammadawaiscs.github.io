Our signature dishes

Our signature dishes are the embodiment of culinary artistry, meticulously crafted to offer an unforgettable dining experience that encapsulates the essence of our restaurant's unique flavors and style.

Saffron-infused Lamb Biryani

Tender pieces of marinated halal lamb are slow-cooked with aromatic spices and layered with fragrant saffron-infused basmati rice. Garnished with fried onions, fresh cilantro, and mint, this dish is a symphony of flavors and textures, offering a taste of culinary sophistication with every forkful.

Harissa Grilled Sea Bass with Mediterranean Couscous

A culinary journey that combines the bold flavors of North Africa with Mediterranean elegance. The sea bass is marinated in fiery harissa, then grilled to perfection, serving as the centerpiece of a bed of fluffy couscous infused with sun-dried tomatoes, olives, and fresh herbs. A drizzle of lemon-garlic sauce completes this exquisite dish.

Berrylicious Dream Cake

Experience fruity enchantment with our 'Berrylicious Dream Cake.' Layers of vanilla sponge brim with juicy strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries, complemented by velvety berry-infused cream. Topped with a gleaming berry glaze, it's pure berry bliss.
